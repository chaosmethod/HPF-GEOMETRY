# HPF Core Axioms

1. Finite Capacity
All regions have bounded update bandwidth.
$0 ≤ Λ ≤ 1$ .

2. Finite Resolution
No infinite precision. No singular states are legal.

3. Locality
All fundamental updates are local.

4. Reversibility
Microscopic evolution is bijective.

5. Conservation
$∇_μ Λ^{μν} = 0$ at the effective level.

---

Interpretation:

Λ = fractional consumption of local update capacity

All higher structures must be derived from these constraints.
